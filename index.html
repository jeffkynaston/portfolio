About

I'm a Certified Management Accountant turned web developer who loves to build software that solves real problems in unique, imaginative ways.

I spent my career in consulting working everywhere from corporate offices to community hospitals to concrete plants and aggregate mines. I've helped small and medium-sized companies develop applications that drive operations, track production data and present financial information. This year, I made the switch full-time into web development because of the impact that more powerful, scalable technology can have on business. My diverse experience has instilled a passion for community and given me a unique perspective on the world that I apply every day while developing software.

I'm always interested in new projects and ideas. Reach me at kynaston.jeffrey@gmail.com.
